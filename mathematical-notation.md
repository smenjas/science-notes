# Mathematical Notation

## Arithmetic
- Addition: `a + b`
- Subtraction: `a - b`
- Multiplication:
  - `a x b`
  - `a * b`
  - `a &middot; b`
- Division:
  - `a:b`
  - `a / b`
  - `a &divide; b`
- Remainder: `3 mod 2 = 1`
- Negative: `-a`
- Exponentiation: `a ^ b`
- Root: `&radic; a`

## Equality
- Equal: `4 = 2 + 2`
- Not Equal: `4 &ne; 1 + 1`
- Identical: `4 &equiv; 2 + 2`
- Approximately Equal: `&pi; &cong; 22/7`

## Comparison
- Less Than: `a < b`
- Greater Than: `a > b`
- Less Than or Equal: `a &lte; b`
- Greater Than or Equal: `a &gte; b`
- Much Smaller Than: `a &Lt; b`
- Much Larger Than: `a &Gt; b`

## Angles
- Pi: `&pi; &cong; 3.14159...`
- Degrees, Minutes, Seconds: `37&deg; 46' 26" N, 122&deg; 25' 53" W`
- Radians: `360&deg; = 2&pi; rad`
